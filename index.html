Legislative Network Graphs

Interactive visualizations of Illinois legislation, categorized by policy area and enriched with LLAMA-extracted features indicating political impact, intended beneficiaries, and increasing/decreasing aspects.

About These Graphs

These network graphs summarize legislative trends that may affect the University of Illinois. Each graph is filtered by political impact, grouped under IGPA policy categories, and includes LLAMA-extracted features describing the bill’s impact on university-related policy domains and beneficiary groups.
